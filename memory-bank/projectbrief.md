# Project Brief: Obsidian Management Starter Vault

## Project Overview
This is an Obsidian vault designed as a comprehensive management toolkit for team leaders, managers, and organizations. It provides structured templates, automated workflows, and organizational systems to support effective team management, member development, and administrative processes.

## Core Requirements
1. **Team Member Management System**
   - Individual team member profiles with comprehensive information tracking
   - Career development path documentation
   - Communication preferences and contact information
   - One-on-one meeting structures and history

2. **Template-Driven Organization**
   - Standardized templates for team member information
   - Callout templates for different types of notes (challenges, highlights, demos, etc.)
   - Periodic note templates (daily/weekly planning)
   - Professional and personal note categorization

3. **Automated Workflow Support**
   - JavaScript scripts for enhanced functionality
   - QuickAdd integration for rapid note creation
   - Dataview queries for dynamic content aggregation
   - Callout system with focus and type selection

4. **Journal and Tracking System**
   - Daily and weekly note structures
   - Progress tracking and review processes
   - Archive system for historical information

## Key Goals
- Streamline team management processes
- Provide consistent documentation standards
- Enable quick access to team member information
- Support career development and performance tracking
- Create scalable organizational structure

## Success Criteria
- Easy onboarding of new team members
- Consistent documentation across all team interactions
- Efficient retrieval of historical information
- Automated workflows reduce manual overhead
- Clear visibility into team member growth and challenges

## Project Scope
- **In Scope**: Template system, automation scripts, organizational structure, team member management
- **Out of Scope**: External integrations, mobile-specific features, real-time collaboration

## Key Stakeholders
- Team managers and leaders
- HR professionals
- Individual team members
- Organizational development teams

## Technical Foundation
- Built on Obsidian knowledge management platform
- Uses JavaScript for automation and enhanced functionality
- Leverages Obsidian plugins: QuickAdd, Dataview, Templater
- Markdown-based for maximum compatibility and longevity
